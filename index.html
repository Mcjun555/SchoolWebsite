Mr. and Ms. Sportsfest 2020

A sportsfest is yearly conducted in the campus. This includes
different sports and traditional Filipino games. The main
highlights of this event would be the pageant and the
cheering competiton. Students from the 3rd grade up to
the 12th are grouped into 4 teams and play against each
other. There are representatives for each event and will
fight for the ranking. The points accumulated in each event
are gathered and the team with the highest points will
be the champion.


Math and Science Quiz Bee

Just like any other schools, educare also conducts these kinds
of competitions, particularly math and science. A group of
three participants will battle out their brains. There are
two departments in the competiton, namely elementary and
high school departments. Three students are chosen each
grade level to join the math quiz bee, and another three
for science quiz bee.
